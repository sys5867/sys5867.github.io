CTP431 HW#2 - Music Visualization

20160347 Sim Yusung

This is a music visualization project for KAIST CTP431 course of 2017 Fall semester.

Choose music from buttons, or stop. Enjoy Visualizations!

Recommended on wide-screen, performance devices.

Concept and idea is from Youtube EDM channels like Monstercat or NoCopyrightSounds.

Used p5.js, p5.sound.js. Amplitude and FFT analyzers are used to extract certain levels from the song and convert into visualizations.
